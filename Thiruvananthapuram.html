About Thiruvananthapuram

Thiruvananthapuram, often referred to as Trivandrum, is the capital city of the southern Indian state of Kerala. It is located on the southwestern tip of the Indian subcontinent, along the Arabian Sea. Thiruvananthapuram is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical significance, and lush green landscapes.
